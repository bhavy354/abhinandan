Natural Sesame Seeds

Description

Our premium natural sesame seeds are carefully selected from the finest crops, ensuring high oil content and rich nutty flavor. These seeds are unprocessed and retain their natural husk, providing maximum nutritional benefits including high levels of calcium, iron, and healthy fats.

Rich in antioxidants and healthy fats
High in calcium, iron, and magnesium
Non-GMO and pesticide-free
Excellent source of plant-based protein

Specifications

Color: Light to dark brown

Moisture: Max 6%

Purity: 99.9%

Packaging: 25kg, 50kg bags or bulk

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. For extended shelf life, store in airtight containers at temperatures below 25°C with relative humidity below 60%. Refrigeration can further extend freshness.

Culinary Uses

Natural sesame seeds are widely used in various culinary applications across different cuisines:

  • Sprinkled on breads, buns, and pastries
  • Key ingredient in tahini paste and halva
  • Used in Asian stir-fries and noodle dishes
  • Added to salads for crunch and nutrition
  • Base for traditional sweets like sesame bars and ladoos
  • Flavoring agent in rice dishes and pilafs

Industrial & Other Uses

Beyond culinary applications, natural sesame seeds have several industrial and health uses:

  • Production of sesame oil for cooking and cosmetics
  • Pharmaceutical preparations and supplements
  • Animal feed ingredient
  • Natural food coloring agent
  • Used in traditional medicine for its health benefits

Origin & Cultivation

Sesame is one of the oldest oilseed crops known to humanity, domesticated over 3,000 years ago. Modern sesame cultivation focuses on high-yield varieties with excellent oil content and flavor profiles.

Growing Regions

The best quality sesame seeds come from these regions:

  • Gujarat and Rajasthan, India - known for high oil content
  • Sudan and Ethiopia - produces premium quality seeds
  • Myanmar - large-scale commercial production
  • China - major global producer
  • Nigeria - significant African producer

Processing Method

Our processing ensures maximum quality and nutrition retention:

  1. Careful selection of premium quality seeds
  2. Thorough cleaning and removal of impurities
  3. Grading by size and color
  4. Quality testing for moisture and oil content
  5. Packaging in moisture-proof materials
  6. Storage in controlled environment
