Onion Flakes

Description

Our premium dehydrated onion flakes are made from fresh, high-quality onions that are carefully cleaned, sliced, and dehydrated to preserve their natural flavor and aroma. These golden-yellow flakes retain the characteristic pungency and sweetness of fresh onions, making them perfect for various culinary applications.

100% natural with no additives
Long shelf life (up to 2 years)
Retains original flavor and nutrients
Quick rehydration properties

Specifications

Color: Golden yellow to light brown

Moisture: Max 8%

Size: 3-5mm flakes

Packaging: 5kg, 10kg, 25kg bags

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep in airtight containers to maintain freshness and prevent moisture absorption. Ideal storage temperature is below 25°C with relative humidity below 60%.

Culinary Uses

Onion flakes are widely used in food processing and culinary applications where fresh onions are impractical. They rehydrate quickly and can be used in:

  • Soups, stews, and sauces
  • Ready-to-eat meals and instant noodles
  • Seasoning blends and spice mixes
  • Meat products and marinades
  • Salad dressings and dips
  • Baked goods and savory snacks

Industrial & Other Uses

Beyond culinary applications, dehydrated onion flakes have several other uses:

  • Pharmaceutical preparations
  • Nutritional supplements
  • Pet food ingredients
  • Natural food coloring

Origin & Cultivation

Onions are one of the oldest cultivated vegetables, with evidence of cultivation dating back over 5,000 years. Modern dehydrated onion products are produced from high-quality onions grown in optimal conditions.

Growing Regions

The best quality onions for dehydration come from these regions:

  • Maharashtra, India - known for high pungency onions
  • Gujarat, India - produces sweet and mild onions
  • California, USA - large-scale commercial production
  • Egypt - known for high dry matter content
  • China - major global producer

Processing Method

Our dehydration process preserves maximum flavor and nutrients:

  1. Selection of premium quality onions
  2. Thorough cleaning and peeling
  3. Precision slicing to uniform thickness
  4. Controlled dehydration at optimal temperatures
  5. Quality testing and sorting
  6. Packaging in moisture-proof materials

Quality Standards

Our dehydrated onion flakes meet international quality standards including ISO, FSSAI, and USDA specifications. We maintain strict hygiene protocols throughout the production process to ensure food safety.
