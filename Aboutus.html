Our Journey Through Time

From humble beginnings to becoming a globally recognized spice exporter

1989

The Foundation

Founded in Unjha, Gujarat by the Patel family with a vision to bring authentic Indian spices to global kitchens. Started with just three spices: cumin, fennel, and coriander.

1995

First International Export

Shipped our first container of premium cumin to the Middle East, establishing our reputation for quality in international markets.

2003

Modern Processing Facility

Invested in state-of-the-art cleaning and sorting equipment, becoming one of the first in the region to implement automated quality control.

2012

Organic Certification

Achieved USDA Organic and EU Organic certifications, expanding our product line to meet growing global demand for organic spices.

2020

Global Recognition

Awarded "Excellence in Spice Export" by the Spices Board of India, now serving over 35 countries across six continents with processing facilities in Gujarat and Andhra Pradesh.
