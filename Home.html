India produces a wide range of spices and holds a prominent position in world spice production. Because of the varying climates from tropical to sub-tropical to temperate, almost all spices grow splendidly in India. Unjha in Gujarat is one such leading market for Indian Spices such as Cumin, Fennel, Fenugreek, Coriander, Mustard, Ajwain, Sesame, Soyabean, and more.
