Desiccated Coconut

Description

Dessicated Coconut is finely grated, dried, and unsweetened coconut meat, made from the white kernel of matured coconuts. Known for its rich texture and natural sweetness, it is widely used in culinary applications across the globe. Our premium-grade dessicated coconut is processed under strict hygiene standards to maintain its purity, freshness, and flavor.

Storage Instructions

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Ideal storage temperature: 15°C to 25°C. Shelf life: Up to 12 months in proper conditions.

Bulk Packaging Options. Private Label & Branding Service. Diverse Packaging Materials. Compliance with International Standards. Sustainable Packaging Options.

Culinary Uses

  • Confectionery (cakes, cookies, biscuits)
  • Indian sweets (laddoos, barfis)
  • Toppings for desserts and ice creams
  • Used in curries, gravies, and chutneys for flavor & texture
  • Ingredient in granola bars, cereals, health snacks
  • Exported for use in bakery & food processing industries

Medicinal & Other Uses

  • Natural Source of Dietary Fiber
  • Provides healthy saturated fats (medium-chain triglycerides - MCTs) for quick energy.
  • Used in exfoliating scrubs and cosmetic formulations as a natural texture agent.
  • Sometimes used in organic shampoos and conditioners for its moisture-retaining properties.

Desiccated Coconut in Different Languages

Desiccated Coconut is known by various names across different cultures and regions:

  • Telugu: Kobbari Podi

Origin & Cultivation

  • Primarily sourced from coastal regions of South India (Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka)
  • Grown in tropical climates with abundant rainfall and rich sandy loam soils
  • Harvested after 12 months of planting
  • Coconuts are de-husked, shelled, pared, and then shredded and dried to achieve fine texture
  • Strict quality checks ensure food safety compliance for international markets
