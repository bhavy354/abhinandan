Mustard Kernels

Description

Our premium mustard kernels are carefully selected from the finest mustard plants, cleaned and processed to maintain their natural pungency and flavor. These golden-yellow kernels are rich in essential oils and are perfect for various culinary applications.

100% natural with no additives
Long shelf life (up to 2 years)
Rich in essential oils
High pungency level

Specifications

Color: Golden yellow

Moisture: Max 8%

Purity: 99.5%

Packaging: 5kg, 10kg, 25kg bags

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep in airtight containers to maintain freshness and prevent moisture absorption. Ideal storage temperature is below 25°C with relative humidity below 60%.

Culinary Uses

Mustard kernels are widely used in food processing and culinary applications where their distinctive flavor and pungency are desired. They can be used in:

  • Pickles and chutneys
  • Curries and gravies
  • Seasoning blends and spice mixes
  • Meat products and marinades
  • Salad dressings and dips
  • Baked goods and savory snacks

Industrial & Other Uses

Beyond culinary applications, mustard kernels have several other uses:

  • Mustard oil production
  • Pharmaceutical preparations
  • Nutritional supplements
  • Natural preservative

Origin & Cultivation

Mustard has been cultivated for thousands of years, with evidence of its use dating back to ancient civilizations. Modern mustard products are produced from high-quality mustard plants grown in optimal conditions.

Growing Regions

The best quality mustard for kernels comes from these regions:

  • Rajasthan, India - known for high oil content
  • Punjab, India - produces large quantities
  • Uttar Pradesh, India - known for quality
  • Canada - major global producer
  • Nepal - known for organic varieties

Processing Method

Our processing method preserves maximum flavor and nutrients:

  1. Selection of premium quality mustard seeds
  2. Thorough cleaning and sorting
  3. Precision dehulling to remove outer shell
  4. Quality grading by size and color
  5. Moisture control and packaging
  6. Quality testing and certification

Quality Standards

Our mustard kernels meet international quality standards including ISO, FSSAI, and USDA specifications. We maintain strict hygiene protocols throughout the production process to ensure food safety and quality.
