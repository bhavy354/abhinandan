Golden Raisins

Description

Golden raisins are made from selected seedless green grapes that are dried to perfection. Our premium golden raisins are treated with sulfur dioxide to preserve their golden color and then dried in controlled conditions. They have a sweet, tangy flavor with a soft, chewy texture, making them perfect for baking, cooking, or snacking.

100% natural with no additives
Rich in iron, potassium and antioxidants
Long shelf life (up to 1 year)
Retains natural sweetness and flavor

Specifications

Color: Golden yellow

Moisture: Max 16%

Size: Medium (300-500 berries/100g)

Packaging: 10kg, 25kg cartons or as per requirement

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry place at temperatures below 25°C with relative humidity below 50%. Keep in airtight containers to maintain freshness and prevent moisture absorption. For long-term storage, refrigeration is recommended. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight and strong odors as raisins can absorb surrounding smells.

Culinary & Nutritional Uses

Golden raisins are versatile ingredients used in various culinary applications and valued for their nutritional benefits:

  • Baking ingredient in cakes, cookies, and breads
  • Salad topping for added sweetness and texture
  • Trail mix component with nuts and seeds
  • Natural sweetener in cereals and yogurt
  • Traditional ingredient in Middle Eastern and Indian cuisine
  • Source of quick energy for athletes and hikers

Health Benefits

Golden raisins offer numerous health benefits due to their rich nutrient profile:

  • Rich in iron which helps prevent anemia
  • Good source of potassium for heart health
  • Contains antioxidants that fight free radicals
  • Natural source of fiber for digestive health
  • Provides calcium and boron for bone strength

Origin & Cultivation

Golden raisins originated in the Mediterranean region and are now produced in several countries with suitable climates. Modern golden raisin production involves careful selection of grape varieties and controlled drying processes to ensure consistent quality.

Growing Regions

The best quality golden raisins come from these regions:

  • California, USA - largest producer with advanced technology
  • Turkey - traditional production methods
  • Iran - premium quality with natural sweetness
  • Afghanistan - organic cultivation
  • India - increasing production in Maharashtra and Karnataka

Processing Method

Our golden raisins are processed using these steps to ensure quality:

  1. Harvesting seedless green grapes at optimal sugar content
  2. Washing and treatment with food-grade sulfur dioxide
  3. Drying in controlled temperature tunnels for 24-48 hours
  4. Mechanical turning for even drying
  5. Cooling and moisture equalization
  6. Cleaning and removal of stems and debris
  7. Grading by size and color uniformity
  8. Packaging in food-grade materials

Quality Standards

Our golden raisins meet international quality standards including ISO, FSSAI, and USDA specifications. We maintain strict hygiene protocols throughout the production process to ensure product safety. All our raisins are tested for moisture content, sugar levels, and absence of contaminants like pesticides and heavy metals.
